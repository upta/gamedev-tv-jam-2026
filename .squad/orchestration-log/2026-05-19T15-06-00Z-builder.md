## Work Items Completed

### NPC AI Behavioral Diversity (D025)

**Problem:** All NPCs behaved identically despite personality weights existing. Same route picks, same ship type (cheapest SD-100), price-only modifications.

**Solution:** Four targeted fixes to `npc_controller.gd`:

1. **Competition-aware route scoring**
   - Candidates scored by `demand - competition_penalty * (1 - slot_aggression)` + distance + jitter
   - Replaces fixed-order iteration
   - Penalizes crowded lanes for cautious NPCs, ignored by aggressive NPCs

2. **Personality-driven ship selection**
   - Aggressive NPCs prefer large ships (high capacity ratio)
   - Cautious NPCs prefer cheap ships (low operating cost)
   - Balanced NPCs match route capacity needs

3. **Route modifications beyond price**
   - Overloaded routes can gain ships (high `route_preference`) or frequency increases
   - Underloaded routes reduce frequency alongside price
   - Dynamic strategy instead of price-only tuning

4. **Per-NPC scoring jitter**
   - ±15% RNG variance breaks ties between identically-weighted candidates
   - Ensures personality differences visible in tie situations
