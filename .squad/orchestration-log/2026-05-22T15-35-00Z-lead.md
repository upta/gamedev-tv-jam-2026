### Decisions Made

**Decision D011: Planet Selection Guide Line** — Merged to `.squad/decisions.md`

**Core design approach:**
- Two-phase click flow: first click enters guide mode (dashed line to cursor), second click emits `route_requested` signal
- Dashed line renders via `_draw()` using Godot's built-in `draw_dashed_line()`, no scene nodes
- Hover panel conditionally shows route distance when snapped to target planet
- `open_with_planets()` method on CreateRouteModal pre-selects both planets
- Guide mode cancels on resize, modal open, or same-planet re-click
- Minimal code footprint: three files modified, no new scenes/classes
