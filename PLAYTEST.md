# Astrobiz — Playtest Guide

## What Is This?

Astrobiz is a galactic airline management game. You run a space transport company, flying passengers and cargo between planets for profit. Three NPC competitors are doing the same. The game runs for 30 turns — highest score wins.

## Getting Started

You begin with:
- **$3,000** in cash
- **1 ship** (a small shuttle with 40 capacity, split 20 passengers / 20 cargo)
- **2 landing slots** (Earth and Mars)

## Core Concepts

### Slots
You need a landing slot at a planet to operate routes there. You start with slots at Earth and Mars. To expand, bid on slots at other planets (costs money, takes 1 turn to process). NPC carriers are also bidding for slots.

### Ships
Ships carry passengers and cargo between planets. Each ship has:
- **Capacity** split between passengers and cargo (you choose the split when ordering)
- **Range** — how far it can fly
- **Build time** — turns until delivery after ordering

You start with a small Shuttle SD-100. Bigger ships unlock at later turns and cost more.

### Routes
A route connects two planets where you own slots. To create a route:
1. Click **Routes** in the top bar
2. Click **Create Route**
3. Pick an origin planet (where you have a slot)
4. Pick a destination planet (where you also have a slot)
5. Assign ships to the route
6. Set frequency (flights per turn — higher = more revenue but more cost)

Routes earn money each turn based on passenger/cargo demand between those planets.

### Demand
Each planet pair has passenger and cargo demand. Hover over planets on the star map to see demand levels (Low/Medium/High). Higher demand = more potential revenue.

## Each Turn

Click **Next Turn** when you're ready to advance. During a turn:
1. Your routes operate and earn/lose money
2. Ship orders and slot bids are processed
3. NPCs take their actions (you'll see a summary of what happened)

## Tips

- Start by creating a route between Earth and Mars (you already have slots there)
- Watch your cash — ships and slots cost money upfront
- Hover over planets on the star map to see demand and slot availability
- Bigger ships and longer routes can earn more, but cost more to operate
- Expand to new systems by bidding on slots at planets in Alpha Centauri, Wolf 359, or Tau Ceti

## Controls

- **Top bar buttons** — Routes, Ships, Slots, Dashboard, Turn Log
- **Star map** — Click planets to select, hover for info
- **Next Turn** — Advances the game one turn
